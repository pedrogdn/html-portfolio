My top four movies of all time

Rudy

Very good inspirational movie about a college football player.

Catch Me If You Can

Based on real events, this movie is amazing. Tells the story of a con artist and a detective who tries to catch him.

Freedom Writers

Also true history, this movie is a drama that shos the harsh reality of students that live in the ghetto area of LA.
